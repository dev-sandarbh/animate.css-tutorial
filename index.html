Welcome to Animate.css Examples

Yesterday, i came across a library which makes it easier for people like me for whom it is difficult to design and make a good user-interface for our students or our clients or even for our own personal projects. So, then Animate.css makes it easy and fun for us to make our content interactive with the help of predefined animations.

So, i have used this css library just for practice and had tried to animate some elements and divs. Here you go. Feel free to share your valuable ideas and comments. Enjoy article.

Items which we can animate are such as buttons specifically CTA's one in order to get customers attention, links which takes us to another important page may be a product page of a site or we can animate a badge given to every product present on the product page. So let us use some animations by applying them onto the different elements.
